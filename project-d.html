Property Description

Ol Saruni Nanyuki offers breathtaking views of the majestic Mount Kenya and Lolldaiga Hills, while being surrounded by nature just a short 15-minute drive from the vibrant Nanyuki town along the Nanyuki-DolDol Road in the Muramati area. Nestled next to Maiyan Resort and opposite Swara Homes, Ol Saruni benefits from an already strong tourism network in the area. Land for sale at Ol Saruni Nanyuki offers a unique blend of modern elegance and natural beauty. The project is an ideal location for Holiday Homes, Airbnb units, and Luxury Hotels!
